How to Operate Unredactor:

To operate this new app, the user types up any sentence (long or short) and to redact the text, the user chooses the words in that sentence to black out. After refreshing the app page or pressing the redact button on the website, the sentence will be edited with the new predicted words substituting the blocked words. This is similiar to "Mad Libs", however, this app predicts the more appropiate words fitted with the context of the sentence versus Mad Libs using unfitting words.
