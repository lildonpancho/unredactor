Unredactor can predict hidden words in a paragraph. Unlike MadLibs which fills a template with random words, Unredactor uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) to guess the most likely word(s) to complete coherent sentences.


Type in your redacted paragraph and use the token unk for hidden words then press Unredact to see the best prediction

Here's an example:

Beyonce is an unk unk and actress

Output: beyonce is an american singer and actress
